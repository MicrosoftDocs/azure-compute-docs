# Customer intent: "As a cloud architect, I want to find out which regions support nonzonal Azure Disk Storage deployments, so that I can plan the deployment of my applications based on availability and redundancy requirements."
---
Currently, Azure supports nonzonal Premium SSD v2 deployments in the following regions:

| *Regions without Availability Zone* | *Three Availability Zones* |
|:-------------------|:-------------------|
| - Australia Central 2 <br> - Australia Southeast <br> - Brazil Southeast <br> - Canada East <br> - Germany North <br> - North Central US <br> - Norway West <br> - South India <br> - Switzerland West <br> - Taiwan North <br> - UK West <br> - US Gov Arizona <br> - West Central US <br> - West US | - Australia East <br> - Brazil South <br> - East Asia <br> - France Central <br> - Germany West Central <br> - Italy North <br> - Korea Central <br> - Mexico Central <br> - New Zealand North <br> - Norway East <br> - Poland Central <br> - Spain Central <br> - South African North <br> - Southeast Asia <br> - Sweden Central <br> - Switzerland North |

To learn when support for particular regions was added, see either [Azure Updates](https://azure.microsoft.com/updates/?query=disk%20storage) or [What's new for Azure Disk Storage](/azure/virtual-machines/disks-whats-new).
