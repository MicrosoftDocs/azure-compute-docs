> [!IMPORTANT]
> Azure Container Instances (ACI) preview features are available on a self-service, opt-in basis. Preview features are provided "as is" and "as available," and are excluded from the service-level agreements (SLAs) and limited warranty. ACI preview features are partially covered by customer support on a best-effort basis. As such, preview features aren't recommended for production workloads and may undergo changes before general availability (GA).
